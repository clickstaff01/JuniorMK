# 01 — Product Overview

## 1.1 Vision

Click Insurance Broker's internal learning and assignment platform — a single place where new hires learn about car insurance, our business, and our channels, then do structured assignments that managers can review, grade, and track over time.

Replaces: Google Docs, Google Sheets, LINE messages for work. Keeps a permanent record for every hire.

## 1.2 Goals

1. Reduce onboarding time variance — every new hire gets the same structured program.
2. Force structured thinking — assignments have templates, so you get comparable answers from everyone.
3. Keep institutional memory — every answer, every grade, every comment is searchable years later.
4. Give the MD a real dashboard — who is struggling, who is ahead, average grade, etc.
5. Reusable for every role — marketing, telesales, claims. Start with Marketing Junior.

## 1.3 Non-goals (v1)

- Not an LMS for external customers. Internal staff only.
- Not a video hosting platform. Embed YouTube / Vimeo if needed.
- Not SCORM-compliant. We are not selling courseware.
- Not a replacement for CRM or payroll.

## 1.4 Personas

### Persona A — Junior Staff ("Nong")
- Age 22–28, 0–2 years experience, some digital marketing familiarity, zero insurance knowledge.
- Uses mobile 50% of the time. UI must be mobile-friendly.
- Primary language: Thai. Reads English at a working level.
- Pain: "I don't know what I don't know. I want to be told what to do each week."

### Persona B — Senior Manager ("Pi")
- 10+ years in insurance. Busy. Reviews assignments in 15-minute windows.
- Wants: fast grading UI, obvious "what's new?" indicator, ability to reuse feedback comments.
- Language: Thai primary.

### Persona C — MD (Owner)
- Ultimate decision maker. Checks the platform maybe once a week.
- Wants: a one-screen dashboard. "Where is each new hire? Who's blocked?"
- Language: Thai and English.

### Persona D — Admin
- Either the MD or an ops person. Adds new courses, new hires, new assignments without writing code.
- Needs: a content editor, a user management page.

## 1.5 Success metrics

| Metric | Target by Month 3 |
|---|---|
| Time to onboard a new hire (start to pass) | ≤ 30 days |
| % of hires who submit every assignment on time | ≥ 85% |
| Average manager grading time per assignment | ≤ 10 minutes |
| Platform uptime | ≥ 99% |
| Thai UI usage | Default; English toggle on demand |

## 1.6 Out-of-scope for MVP (but on roadmap)

- Peer review (junior reviews another junior's answer)
- Gamification (points, badges)
- SSO with Google Workspace
- Mobile native app (web is responsive for now)
- AI-assisted first-pass grading
